Mission & Vision

Safer, smarter, inclusive parking

Drivers today struggle to find available parking, leading to traffic congestion, wasted fuel, and lost time across busy campuses and cities. Our mission is to solve this by unifying Computer Vision and IoT into a single intelligent layer.

We provide a seamless system that helps drivers find and access parking quickly with automated gates and live spot availability. Beyond convenience, our system ensures 24/7 continuous monitoring through advanced YOLOv8 models, keeping every vehicle under watch and detecting hazards or unauthorized access in real time.

Most importantly, our infrastructure is inclusive by design, featuring smart-managed parking spots dedicated to people with special needs, ensuring fair, reliable, and respectful access for everyone.

Core Features

What our system provides

Automated Gate Access

Seamless entry and exit powered by IoT-controlled barriers, verifying authorized vehicles in real time.

License Plate Recognition

High-accuracy OCR pipelines identify and log vehicles instantly, supporting frictionless access and audit trails.

Tire Safety & Hazard Detection

Custom-trained YOLOv8 models analyze live camera feeds to flag worn tires and on-site hazards before they escalate.

Accessibility Support

Dedicated smart parking spots and accessible routing ensuring inclusive and prioritized access for people with disabilities.

Accident Detection

Immediate AI-driven detection and automated alerts for any in-lot collisions, ensuring rapid response to emergencies.

Dynamic Pricing

Automated fare adjustments based on real-time demand, parking availability, and vehicle stay duration.
