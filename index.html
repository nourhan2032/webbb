Core Technologies

A comprehensive ecosystem combining precise processing, rapid response, and the highest safety standards.

Real-Time Detection (YOLO)

Instant AI analysis of surveillance feeds to pinpoint vacant and occupied spaces with ultra-high accuracy.

License Plate Recognition (ALPR)

Seamless and automated entry and exit management through smart license plate reading without human intervention.

Live Data Analytics

Full data synchronization providing real-time occupancy rates and insights to drive informed decision-making.

Incident & Accident Detection

Smart detection of collisions or breakdowns inside the facility, triggering instant alerts for rapid management intervention.

Automated Tire Inspection

AI-driven tire condition analysis upon entry to alert drivers of severe wear or potential issues, ensuring maximum safety.

Smart Accessible Parking

Dedicated monitoring for spaces assigned to people with disabilities, actively issuing alerts for unauthorized parking.
